Self-Rating and Gender

From the survey results, it is seen that the gender of the majority who answered the survey is the female at 71%, 37 compared to the male’s at 29%, 15 out of 52. So, do be warned that the results may be biased more toward the women’s side rather than the men’s side. But, this project is just an experiment using sampling methods, only testing as number of the total population (Grade 9 students). Now, let us look into self-ratings, we can see that the majority chose 8 at 25%, 13 out of 52. And the values that got the least votes were numbers, 1 and 2, at 0%, 0 out of 52. Using the chart, 25% of the sample size chose 5 and below while the other 75% chose 6 and up.
